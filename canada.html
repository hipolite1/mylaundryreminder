How it works in Canada

1) Subscribe

Pick a plan and access your portal.

2) Enter pickups

Staff add name, mobile, and due date; choose SMS/Call.

3) Daily reminders

On/after the due date, we send one reminder per day.

4) Stop on pickup

Customer replies DONE or you mark picked—loop stops.
