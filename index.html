FAQ

Does this replace our CRM?

No. MyLaundryReminder is a pickup-reminder layer. Keep your POS/CRM — we only handle the daily reminder loop.

Which channels are supported?

SMS and Call at launch. WhatsApp can be added later.

How do reminders stop?

Customer taps the link in any reminder or replies DONE (for SMS). Staff can also mark “Picked” in the portal.

Do you support quiet hours?

Yes—set execution windows so messages send only during business hours.
