    FAQ

    Does this replace our CRM?

    No. MyLaundryReminder is a pickup‑reminder layer. Keep your POS/CRM — we only handle the daily reminder loop.

    What channels can we use?

    SMS, WhatsApp, and/or Call. Pick one or multiple; you can set a default and override per customer.

    How do reminders stop?

    Customer taps the link in any reminder or replies DONE. Staff can also mark “Picked” in the portal.

    Are you compliant?

    Yes — every message identifies the business and includes STOP/HELP. Opt‑in logs and quiet hours are supported.
