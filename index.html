How it works

This is the exact workflow your staff will use.

1Add a customer

Enter name + phone. Done.

2Add a pickup

Set due date for garments ready for pickup.

3We remind daily

One reminder per day until pickup is completed.

4Mark picked up

Reminders stop instantly when status changes.

FAQ

Does the customer need an app?

No. They receive SMS/Call reminders automatically.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You can cancel from the portal (billing management) or by contacting support.

What stops reminders?

Once a pickup is marked “picked up,” reminders stop automatically.
