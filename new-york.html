How it works

Simple A→Z for your team:

  • ✓ Add a pickup in the portal (name, phone, Due Date).
  • ✓ On the Due Date (e.g., 10:00 AM local), customer gets SMS.
  • ✓ If not picked up, we send one SMS per day.
  • ✓ Customer replies DONE or staff marks Picked — reminders stop.
  • ✓ Works with your current POS/CRM; no migration.
