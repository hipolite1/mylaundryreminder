Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 5, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Quenex Canada Inc. (doing business as mylaundryreminder.com) (“Company,” “we,” “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use MyLaundryReminder (the “Service”).

Not legal advice. Consider asking counsel to review for your jurisdiction and use case.

1) Information we collect

  • Account & business info: name, email, phone, business details.
  • Customer info you submit: names, phone numbers, due dates, and message content needed to send reminders.
  • Billing info: handled by Stripe (card brand/last4, billing address), and subscription status.
  • Device & usage data: IP address, browser, pages viewed, timestamps, referral/UTM, and interactions with the portal/pages.
  • Support communications: emails, chats, and call notes.
  • Logs: message delivery/meta from our messaging provider(s).

2) How we use information

  • Provide and operate the Service (including sending SMS reminders).
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions.
  • Authenticate, secure, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot.
  • Analyze usage to improve features and reliability.
  • Provide customer support and communicate changes.
  • Comply with legal obligations and carrier rules.

3) Sharing & processors

We share information with trusted service providers who process data on our behalf, including:

  • Payments: Stripe (card processing, invoicing, customer portal)
  • Messaging: LeadConnector and underlying carriers for SMS/voice delivery
  • Hosting & analytics: our web hosting/CDN and analytics tools
  • Support & ops: email and ticketing platforms

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and the integrity of the Service, and in a business transfer (e.g., merger/acquisition).

4) SMS consent, opt-out, frequency (including CASL)

  • We send SMS only to recipients who have provided consent to the dry cleaner using the Service.
  • Each message includes opt-out instructions. Reply STOP to end messages; HELP for help. Message and data rates may apply.
  • Message frequency varies (typically a due-date reminder, then one per day until pickup).
  • For Canadian recipients, we follow Canada’s Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL). Dry cleaners using the Service must obtain valid express or implied consent and maintain consent records.

5) Cookies & tracking

We may use cookies and similar technologies to provide core functionality (e.g., login), remember preferences, and measure performance. You can control cookies via your browser settings; essential features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

6) Data retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to provide the Service and for legitimate business purposes (e.g., security, billing, legal compliance). We may anonymize and aggregate data for analytics.

7) Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect personal data. No system is 100% secure; please use strong, unique passwords and protect your credentials.

8) Your choices & rights

  • You may access, update, or delete certain account information via the portal or by contacting us.
  • End-customers can opt out of SMS at any time by replying STOP.
  • Depending on your location, you may have additional rights (e.g., to request access/erasure). Contact us to exercise rights: info@mylaundryreminder.com.

9) International transfers

We may process data in Canada, the United States, and other countries where our providers operate. Where applicable, we use appropriate safeguards for cross-border transfers.

10) Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or under the minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

11) Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are material, we will provide reasonable notice (e.g., email or site banner). Your continued use after the effective date signifies acceptance.
